About Me

My name is Blake Keller. I am married to my beautiful wife Jordyn. We have two toy australian shepherds, a boy named Vader and a girl named Cinnamon. They are pretty much our kids and we would do anything for them.

I attended Weber State University to play college football for the Wildcats. I received my bachelor's degree in Electronic Media Communications. I originally wanted to get into sports broadcasting, but my life just ended up taking me in a different direction.

For the last 5 years I have been working for Federal Express. I started out in customer service and have since become a driver for them, which I much prefer. During the fall the last 5 years, I have also coached football at Herriman High School. We won the 5a state championship in 2015.

Unfortunately, this year I am not going to continue to coach. I have decided to take up this bootcamp class in order to change careers and have more opportunities. I have two friends who work in the computer programming field and I can't wait to join them.
